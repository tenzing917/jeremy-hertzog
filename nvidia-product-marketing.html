How I Think About Product Marketing

The hardest part of product marketing at a platform company is maintaining coherence. Every product team has its own roadmap, its own technical language, its own idea of what matters most. The PMM's job is to connect all of that into a story that makes sense to someone on the outside, whether they're evaluating a single product or trying to understand the whole platform. You start with what the product does today, not what the roadmap promises. You figure out who cares and why, in their language, not yours. And you build from there. The messaging framework is the foundation, not the deliverable. The deliverable is a field team that can explain the value confidently, a partner ecosystem that can extend the story into their own markets, and customers who can articulate why they chose you. Everything else is just artifacts.
