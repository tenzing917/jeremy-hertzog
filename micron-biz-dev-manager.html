Questions I'd Ask in My First Week
  • What does the current account strategy look like, and where are the gaps we already know about?
  • How does customer feedback get back to product planning today, and where does it get stuck?
  • What does the competitive landscape look like in this account right now, and where are we gaining or losing ground?
How I Think About This Work

Business development isn't sales. It's strategic matchmaking, finding where the customer's roadmap and your product roadmap overlap and building the bridge between them. The best BDMs listen more than they pitch. They bring customer truth back to the product team and product possibility forward to the customer. I think in frameworks like TAM, SOM, and competitive positioning maps, but I also trust the instinct that comes from sitting across the table from buyers for years.

How I'd Want to Be Held Accountable
  • Account revenue growth and market share gain.
  • Quality of the feedback loop between customers and the product roadmap.
  • Forecast accuracy.
  • Whether cross-functional partners actually want to work with me.
